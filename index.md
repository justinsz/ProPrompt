## 🎯 What's Inside?

Each guide includes:

- **Difficulty levels** from ⭐ Easy to ⭐⭐⭐ Hard
- **Prompt examples** – copy-paste ready for your daily work
- **Agent examples** – Copilot Studio & VS Code Agent Mode
- **Mermaid diagrams** – process flows, toolchains, architectures
- **Cheat sheets** – quick reference templates & context checklists

## 🏁 Quick Start

1. **Pick your language** – Deutsch or English
2. **Read the Fundamentals** – understand the RICE framework
3. **Choose your job guide** – Analysts, Legal, Developers, or Office
4. **Copy the templates** – use the Cheat Sheet in your daily work
